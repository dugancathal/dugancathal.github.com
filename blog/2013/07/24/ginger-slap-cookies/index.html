Ginger Slap Cookies

Jul 24th, 2013 | Comments

These cookies are originally from a recipe book I found in a thrift store. They’ve since become a staple at any party we have or attend. I have, however, made one modification. The cayenne that is called for was actually supposed to be jalapeño powder, but as I could never find that at any store I normally go to, I made the swap and haven’t looked back.

Enjoy these with a nice cup of Mexican Hot Chocolate for a really nice compliment.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Egg

  • 1 stick of butter

  • 1 cup white sugar

  • ½ tsp vanilla

  • 2 tsp milk

  • 2 tsp ground ginger

  • 2 tsp cayenne pepper

  • 2 tsp baking powder

  • 1 ¾ cups white flour

Instructions

Combine the ingredients in the order listed. Then drop and bake on a cookie sheet at 350ºF for about 9 minutes; you want them slightly chewy.
